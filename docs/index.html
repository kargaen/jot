Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Quick Capture

Global hotkey summons a capture bar. Type, hit enter, keep moving. The thought is saved before you lose it.

Spaces & Projects

Organize by area and project. Color-coded, filterable, zero overhead. Structure that helps, not hinders.

Today's Pulse

A gentle reminder window shows what's due. Always-on-top, auto-dismissing, out of your way when you're in the zone.

Logbook

Completed tasks don't vanish. They're celebrated with a heatmap and a little encouragement. You earned it.

Due Dates & Overdue

Overdue tasks surface automatically. No manual triage, no guilt-tripping — just clear visibility.

Native & Fast

Built with Tauri. Starts instantly, uses minimal resources, lives in your system tray. Not another Electron app.

Philosophy

Built with intent.

  • 01 Speed is a feature. If the app is slower than pen and paper, it's failed.
  • 02 No busywork. No labels, no priorities, no kanban boards. Just tasks, dates, and done.
  • 03 Your data, synced. Cloud sync across devices. Your tasks follow you.
  • 04 Stay in flow. Quick capture from anywhere. Reminders that know when you're idle. No interruptions.
